The Golden Rules

Never stop learning

Never back down, never give up. It will seem to be hard but you just need to work.

Be unchaseable

Be very carefull with your addresses online and with every information that you give to the world.

Take a break

If you feel that things are not going well you might need to take a break.


Become a white hacker or an ethical hacker

What is a white hacker or an ethical hacker you might ask. Well when hackers appeared they were just a bunch of people with the goal to make fun of others and steal their data. The terms of white hacker and ethical hacker means that you are a good hacker not a bad one. This sort of jobs are highly paid in companies and as far as today companies want more and more people on the cybersecurity team so you will not regret it. Everytime you will need to make a decision for your own life so try to make a good one and don't accept the temptation.
